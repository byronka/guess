
Instructions: 
------------

Pick a number in your mind between 1 and 1000.  
Now, type "ready" at the prompt, like this:

 > ready

and then press the Enter key.

The computer will try to guess your number.  Each time, You must type
"higher" or "lower", and it will offer new guesses.

If the computer has guessed the proper number, type "yes" at the
prompt, then press enter.

 > yes

If you wish to end before the computer has guessed your number, type
"end" at the prompt, then press enter.

 > end

If you enter "higher" or "lower" incorrectly, just type "oops" and it
will give you the previous query.
